What is stapled Visa
If the stapled visa is issued to someone, the immigration Officer does not stamp on the passport of the passenger/applicant. Rather an additional paper is attached to the passport of the passenger. The attached additional paper has the full detail of the trip like the purpose and duration of the trip in the destination country. So instead of a passport, this paper is stamped by the officials called Stapled Visa.
